üìñ How to Fix in Google Console

  1. Go to Google Cloud Console - Credentials
  2. Find your OAuth 2.0 Client ID and click on it
  3. Under "Authorized redirect URIs", click "ADD URI"
  4. Paste: http://localhost:3000/api/auth/google/callback
  5. Under "Authorized JavaScript origins", click "ADD URI"
  6. Paste: http://localhost:3000
  7. Click "SAVE" at the bottom
  8. Wait 10-30 seconds for changes to take effect
  9. Come back here and click "Test Google Login"
